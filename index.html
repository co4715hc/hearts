Directions and Rules
Goal

Avoid receiving the queen of spades and any hearts cards.

Have the lowest score at the end of the game.

Phases

Pass: At the start of each round, you must choose three cards to pass to another player. Every fourth round there will be no passing.

Trick: During the trick phase, you must choose a valid card to discard every turn.

Rules

The player with the two of clubs must begin each round with that card.

You cannot play a queen of spades or a hearts card on the first trick of a round.

You must match the leading card's suit if you have a matching card.

If you don't have a matching card, you can play any card.

The player with the highest value card of the leading suit takes the trick.

Shooting the moon: If one player has all 13 hearts and the queen of spades at the end of a round, they add 0 points to their score and each other player receives 26.

Points

Every hearts card is worth 1 point.

Every queen of spades is worth 13 points.
